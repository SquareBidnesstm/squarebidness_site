Shipping Policy

Last updated: Oct 13, 2025

✅ Free standard shipping on all U.S. orders.

We aim to ship orders quickly and reliably. The details below apply to all orders placed on squarebidness.com.

Methods & Carriers

  • Free standard, tracked shipping via USPS, UPS, or FedEx (carrier selected at our discretion).
  • Tracking information is emailed when your label is created.
  • Signature confirmation may be required for higher-value orders.

Rates

  • Free standard shipping on all U.S. orders.
  • Expedited options may appear during checkout when available (carrier surcharges apply).

Handling & Transit Times

  • Handling: 1–3 business days.
  • Transit (U.S.): typically 3–7 business days.
  • Peak seasons, carrier constraints, or weather may extend timelines.

Preorders

VSOP Preorder Ships by Nov 1, 2025.

  • Orders containing preorder items may ship together by the preorder date to reduce emissions and costs.
  • If you need in-stock items sooner, please place separate orders.

PO Boxes & Address Accuracy

  • PO Boxes are supported via USPS; some carriers require a street address.
  • Please confirm your address at checkout. We aren’t responsible for issues caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses.

International Shipping

We currently ship within the United States only. We’re working to expand coverage.

Delays, Lost, or Damaged Packages

  • If tracking shows delivered but you can’t locate the package, check with neighbors and your local carrier first.
  • Report issues to us within 7 days of the delivery scan (or expected date if lost) so we can investigate and file a carrier claim.
  • For damaged items, keep all packaging and include photos in your email.

Returns & Exchanges

Returns are handled under our Returns Policy (30 days on unworn items). For preorder cancellations, see the preorder section on the returns page.
