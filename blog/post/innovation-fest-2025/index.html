Innovation Fest 2025: From Louisiana to NYC

Four days in New York showing Square Bidness to a bigger room — funding convos, retail interest, and keeping the story tied to Louisiana the whole time.

We didn’t pull up to Innovation Fest to just be in the building. We came to show proof of work — Louisiana-built brand, premium materials, and a founder who can speak funding, fulfillment, and fashion in the same breath. That hits different in NYC.

“If it says Square Bidness, you can wear it anywhere — Louisiana to Manhattan — and still look ready to handle business.”

What we really came to do

We kept it in three lanes:

  • Buyers & boutiques: tap people who like limited allocation, not race-to-the-bottom volume.
  • Manufacturing & equipment: make sure the multi-head embroidery push is understood as an expansion, not a pivot.
  • Capital & partners: meet the folks who want to back a real brand — veteran-owned, Louisiana-rooted, America-first energy.

What landed in the room

  • Material story: Supima + disciplined QC made people slow down and actually ask about sourcing.
  • Embroidery capability: “You’re buying equipment to take on outside contract work too?” — that got attention.
  • Distribution: we said “selective wholesale” and it made sense — preserve brand, don’t overexpose.
  • Founder presence: Louisiana cadence + NYC delivery. People remembered it.

Why NYC was the right room

New York tests your story. If it’s thin, it breaks. Ours didn’t. We talked Hammond, Pineville, the property on N Baptist Rd, and the VSOP drop the same way we talk it at home — and it still translated. That’s how we know the brand travels.

Innovation Fest — quick FAQ

What did we go to NYC to accomplish?

Show the work, meet decision makers, and connect with capital that respects craftsmanship and real growth.

We’re pushing equipment and fulfillment so your orders move faster and stay premium. That’s the whole play.

Yes — Champion, Saucony, heritage streetwear, travel brands — but only when the values line up.

What’s next?

Follow-ups from the fest, finalizing equipment, tightening the drop calendar (Liberty → VSOP → winter story), and pushing Square Bidness Tech Lab content.
