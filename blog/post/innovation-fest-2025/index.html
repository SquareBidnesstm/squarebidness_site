Innovation Fest 2025: From Louisiana to NYC

Four days in New York City at one of the biggest innovation conferences in the country. Here's what I learned about taking Square Bidness to the next level.

Just wrapped up Innovation Fest 2025 in NYC, and I'm still processing everything that happened. Coming from Louisiana, networking with tech leaders, entrepreneurs, and innovators from around the world — it's my first time in NYC and in this kind of environment, and I loved every minute of it.

Why Innovation Fest Mattered

When I first heard about Innovation Fest, I knew Square Bidness needed to be there. Not just as attendees, but as participants. We're not just building a clothing brand — we're building a movement rooted in principles, morals, and business done right.

The conference brought together people who are changing industries, disrupting norms, and building for the future. That's exactly where Square Bidness belongs.

Key Takeaways

Representation Matters

Walking into rooms where you're often the only person who looks like you, comes from where you come from, or represents what you represent — it's powerful and necessary. Square Bidness isn't just about apparel. It's about showing that Louisiana has innovators, that veterans can build businesses, that principles can drive profit.

The Power of Authentic Networking

I met founders, investors, and industry leaders who were genuinely interested in our story. Not because we had the flashiest pitch deck, but because our mission resonated. When you're building something real, people feel it.

“Principles. Morals. Business Done Right. That's not just our tagline — it's how we showed up in every conversation.”

What's Next for Square Bidness

The connections made at Innovation Fest are already opening doors. We're exploring partnerships that could take our distribution national, investors who understand our vision, and mentors who've walked similar paths.

But more than that, the experience reinforced that we're on the right track. The VSOP Collection launch timing couldn't be better. The market is ready for brands that stand for something deeper than just products.

Louisiana to the World

One thing that stood out: people were fascinated by our Louisiana roots. In a world of generic brands and copy-cat strategies, authenticity cuts through the noise. Our story — veteran-owned, Louisiana-based, principle-driven — that’s not something you can manufacture.

The VSOP Connection

While at the conference, I made it to Moët Hennessy's offices. Standing there in VSOP-inspired pieces, right next to the Moët Hennessy sign in there U.S.A. headquarters, it hit me — this is what the collection represents. Very Superior. Original Principles. Not just in cognac, but in business, in life, in how we show up.

What Innovation Really Means

Innovation isn't just about tech or disruption. Sometimes it's about taking timeless principles and applying them in new ways. Square Bidness innovates by prioritizing values in an industry that often doesn't. We innovate by being authentic in a space full of manufactured personas.

Square Bidness. Principles. Morals. Business Done Right.
