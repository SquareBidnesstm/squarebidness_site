Square Bidness does not publish physical street addresses on any public-facing website, asset, or metadata.

This includes:
- HTML content
- Meta tags
- JSON-LD
- Open Graph / Twitter cards
- Images (OG, hero, previews)
- Dev/test pages
- Analytics or diagnostic pages

Approved location references are limited to:
- City + State
- Region (Northshore, Central Louisiana, etc.)
- State-only

Physical addresses are considered **private operational data** and may only be used in:
- Internal documents
- Lender packets
- Legal filings
- Private contracts

Any public deployment containing a physical address is considered a policy violation and must be removed immediately.
