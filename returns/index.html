Returns & Exchanges

Business done right — if it isn’t right, we’ll help make it right.

Effective date: September 29, 2025 • Contact: squarebidnessapparel@gmail.com

1) Overview

  • Return window: 30 days from delivery
  • Condition: Unworn, unwashed, with original tags/packaging
  • Non-refundable: Original/expedited shipping, duties/taxes outside the U.S.
  • Return shipping: Customer pays unless we made an error or item is defective

2) Preorders (VSOP Collection)

  • Cancel before shipment: Full refund
  • After shipment: Standard 30-day policy applies
  • Sizing exchanges: Processed as return → refund/credit → new order (keeps inventory accurate)

3) Eligibility Checklist

  • New condition (no wear, wash, odors, or damage)
  • All tags and branded packaging included
  • Proof of purchase (order # / confirmation email)

We may deny returns that do not meet these criteria.

4) Non-returnable Items

  • Final-sale or clearance items marked “Final Sale”
  • Gift cards or digital products
  • Customized or altered items
  • Used/washed items or items missing tags/packaging

5) Defects or Wrong Item

If your order arrived defective or incorrect, email us within 7 days of delivery. Include photos of the full item, the issue, and your packing slip. We’ll replace or refund and cover return shipping where required.

6) Refund Timing

Refunds go to the original payment method after your return is received and inspected (typically 3–7 business days after it reaches us). You’ll receive an email once processed.
