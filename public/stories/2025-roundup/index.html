2025 Round Up — Greatness Is a Process

From Louisiana buildouts to New York boardrooms — 2025 showed Square Bidness can stand anywhere.

January 1, 2026 · By Marcus Buckner

The year started fast.

I came out of 2024 deep in the infrastructure of HQ — but I wasn’t as hands-on as I am now. That changed quick.

On January 1, I pulled up to HQ1, met up with an electrician partner, and got some lights back on that had been out. As soon as those lights came on, it was on. I ordered LVP, got the flooring delivered, and locked in. I covered HQ1 and HQ2, laid down butcher block countertops, and really started treating the space like a true production base — not just somewhere I stored boxes.

From there, I went deeper. I started learning the electrical trade from my amigo and my homeboy Jarvis. What began as “let me just get a little more light in here” turned into a whole lot of light — recessed lighting everywhere, ceiling fans (72s, 66s, 60s), and upgrades that strengthened the foundation for Square Bidness Apparel to actually run the right way.

I knew the days of running back and forth without proper infrastructure couldn’t go on forever. So I went and put property under Square Bidness. That was the shift: from “I got some shirts” to “I own the ground this brand stands on.”

Leaving Louisiana, expanding the vision

For a few years, I hadn’t really left Louisiana. 2025 broke that.

I hit Las Vegas, around other fashion designers — listening, soaking up game, seeing how people move in different markets.

Then came San Francisco, and that’s where everything really changed. I got introduced to the world of coding and saw how the internet, infrastructure, and software actually move money and opportunity. That changed everything.

From there, an invite to New York shifted how I saw the world. Standing in the fashion capital, I realized: I can hold my own anywhere. Louisiana to NYC — I stood on all ten.

I made it to D.C. for a world-changing ideas summit and realized I’d been behind on certain things — but that was past tense. I caught up quick. Those trips showed me it’s not just about selling clothes. It’s about:

Systems.
Infrastructure.
Values.
Mission.

That’s where 2025 really leveled up.

Process, principles, and 2026

One thing I know for sure: There is a process to all of this. God first. You cannot cheat the process. You can’t skip the line if you want what you’re building to last.

People see someone else doing something and think, “I can do that too.” Then when they don’t get the same results, they start asking, “Why him? I can do that.”

But here’s what I learned: perfect something first. Lock in on one lane, build it solid, then move over. If you skip that, you don’t just get in somebody else’s way — you get in your own way.

I caught a slogan at the Paper Planes store that stuck with me:

Greatness is a process.

That’s exactly how this feels.

Four years in — still in motion

Square Bidness was founded in January 2022. Four years later, we’re still at the top of our game — not because it was easy, but because we stayed in motion:

• Building HQ infrastructure.
• Owning property in the brand’s name.
• Traveling and learning from Vegas, San Francisco, New York, D.C., and Dallas.
• Launching Square Bidness Tech Lab.
• Stepping into media, storytelling, and digital systems.
• Getting invited inside banks, talking funding, and deciding who we want to partner with.

Now it’s 2026 — and we’re here to stay.

We’re building a team. We’re creating jobs. We’re getting welcomed into rooms we used to only dream about — and we’re walking in as Square Bidness, not just asking for a seat, but bringing value to the table.

So get ready.

2026, we scaling and saling — standing on the foundation I built four years ago. Get with it, not without it. Square Bidness.
