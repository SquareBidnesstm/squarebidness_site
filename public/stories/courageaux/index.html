From Amari

It’s an HONOR , to be Recognized by my city. Courageaux Aesthetics came from Joshua 1:9 be courageous for the lord is with you where ever you go. When I left school in New Orleans i understood the demand for skin care in Hammond wasn’t really a thing. Many people thought I couldn’t make money doing this , but I love what I do and my clients recognize it. I was put in position to show people of color we deserve luxury too. Reading text books that didn’t have people that look like me made me want to show the world that corrective skin care IS for us! When I became licensed I promised God that I will handle my Bidness !!

— Amari Wilford · Inspiration: Joshua 1:9
Inspiration: Joshua 1:9 — “Be courageous.” A calm space. A clear process. A standard of luxury for people of color.
Bio

Amari Wilford is the licensed esthetician behind Courageaux Aesthetics in Hammond, Louisiana. Her approach is calm, disciplined, and results-driven — focused on barrier health, simple routines, and professional care you can feel.

Courageaux serves clients across the Northshore and broader Southeastern Louisiana, with a focus on corrective skin care for people of color who deserve luxury, education, and consistency.
