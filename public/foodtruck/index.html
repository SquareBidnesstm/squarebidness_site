QR Menu

Customers scan your card, flyer, table sign, or window decal and land directly on your ordering page.

Prepaid Checkout

Orders are paid before they hit the truck. Less chasing cash, fewer fake orders, cleaner pickup flow.

Live Order Screen

Your team sees paid orders in one place with customer name, phone, pickup time, items, notes, and status.

Operator Controls

Pause ordering, force open, update menu sections, and mark items sold out without rebuilding the site.

Catering Requests

Separate catering intake for bigger jobs, events, deposits, and follow-up outside the pickup menu.

Event Ready

Festival menus, weekend specials, and temporary item changes can be handled without confusing customers.
