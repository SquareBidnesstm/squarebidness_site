Press bio

Nae Doll — born Jerenia Baham — has been riding since she could balance.

Raised in Ponchatoula, Louisiana, she built her name through discipline, fearlessness, and consistency. It was never about attention or trends — it was always about the love of it.

What started as instinct became identity.

Ponchatoula made her solid.
The bike made her sharp.

At 24, she stands in her prime — grounded, controlled, and fully in command of her presence. Whether she’s riding or stepping into a frame, the energy stays the same: intentional, unforced, undeniable.

No gimmicks.
No noise.
Just presence.

Still ridin.
