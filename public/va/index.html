Main script

I appreciate you meeting with me. I want to be upfront about why I’m here. My service-connected conditions make traditional employment very difficult for me to sustain. My VA decision already found that I am unable to secure or follow substantially gainful employment because of my PTSD, and the record also shows that I have difficulty functioning around others, difficulty accepting supervision, trouble staying focused, and low energy and motivation. That lines up with my real experience. A traditional work setting, especially one with close supervision, a lot of interaction, or a rigid structure, is not the right fit for me. It creates problems for my focus, my stress level, and my ability to stay consistent long-term. What I have been building is a different path that works better with my limitations. I am building Square Bidness, which is a business focused on digital infrastructure for independent operators. I build systems that help restaurants and service businesses organize demand, payments, intake, and operations more cleanly. What I’m looking for through VR&E is a rehabilitation path that makes sense for my actual conditions. I am interested in being evaluated for the self-employment track because self-employment gives me the ability to work in a controlled environment, manage my workflow, reduce triggers, and build stable income in a way that is more realistic for me than traditional employment. I am not coming in with just an idea. I have already taken real steps, built real systems, and started creating real-world business activity. What I need now is support to make that path more stable, more sustainable, and better aligned with my disability limitations. I’d like to understand what the next steps are to evaluate me for the self-employment track, what documentation you need from me, and what the process looks like for moving toward a formal rehabilitation plan built around this direction.

Short answers

Fast responses

Use these when they ask direct questions quickly.

Why can’t you work a regular job?

Because my condition affects exactly the parts of work that a regular job depends on most. My VA record already reflects problems with functioning around others, accepting supervision, staying focused, and maintaining energy and motivation. I’m trying to build a work structure that matches my reality instead of forcing myself into one that I already know is not sustainable.

Why self-employment?

Self-employment gives me more control over my environment, workflow, pace, and triggers. That matters for me. It gives me the best chance to stay productive and build stable income in a way that works with my disability instead of against it.

What is your goal?

My goal is to use self-employment as a realistic rehabilitation path that allows me to work in a controlled environment, accommodate my disability, and build stable income.

What track are you seeking?

I want to be evaluated for the VR&E self-employment track because it is the direction that best fits my actual work barriers and limitations.

Positioning

What to say about Square Bidness

Keep it operational and real.

What is your business?

My business is Square Bidness. I build digital infrastructure for independent operators, especially restaurants and service businesses. That means helping them organize ordering, payments, customer intake, and operations into cleaner systems. The focus is not just websites or branding. It’s building real operational systems that reduce chaos and help businesses function better.

Is this just an idea?

No. I’m already doing it. I have already built and launched live systems and have real-world execution behind what I’m saying. What I need now is help turning that into a more sustainable rehabilitation path that fits my disability limitations.

Core business line

I build systems for independent operators so they can organize demand, take payments cleanly, and operate with less chaos.

How to frame your progress

I’m not coming in empty-handed. I’ve already taken steps, built real systems, and created live business activity. I’m asking for the right rehabilitation structure to make that path sustainable.

The ask

What you want from VR&E

Keep this broad at first and stay out of shopping-list mode.

What you want

First, I want to be evaluated for the self-employment track. Second, I want to understand what plan, documentation, and milestones are required to make this my formal rehabilitation path. Third, if I am approved for that direction, I want support that helps me build a sustainable work setup consistent with my limitations, whether that includes training, planning, equipment, or other approved services.

If they push regular employment first

I understand why that would normally be considered, but in my case my VA record already supports that traditional substantially gainful employment has not been sustainable because of my service-connected condition. I’m asking for a rehabilitation path that is more realistic for my actual barriers. Self-employment is the direction that best fits those barriers.

What not to do

  • Do not open with equipment
  • Do not sound like you only want funding
  • Do not oversell or get dramatic
  • Do not argue more than necessary

Counselor questions

What you should ask back

These questions keep the call moving in the right lane.

Ask these

Based on my VA findings and current limitations, what is the process for being evaluated for the self-employment track? What documentation would you want from me to show that self-employment is the most realistic rehabilitation path in my case? If that direction is approved, what are the next formal steps toward building an individualized rehabilitation plan around it?
