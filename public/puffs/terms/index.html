SMS Terms & Conditions

Puff’s Smokehouse
Program name: Puff’s Smokehouse Transactional Alerts
Effective Date: February 5, 2026

By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive transactional SMS messages from Puff’s Smokehouse related to your order. These messages may include payment confirmations, order status updates, and pickup notifications.

Message frequency varies depending on order activity. Message and data rates may apply.

No marketing or promotional messages are sent via SMS.

How to Opt In

Customers opt in by checking an unchecked consent checkbox during checkout on https://www.squarebidness.com/puffs/. You can view the consent checkbox example here: https://www.squarebidness.com/puffs/sms-optin/. Phone number is optional. If you do not opt in, we will not text you.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages at any time.
