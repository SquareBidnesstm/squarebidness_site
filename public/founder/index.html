Founder Bio

Press-ready summary · Copy/paste safe for editors and partners.

Marcus Buckner is the founder of Square Bidness Apparel and Square Bidness Tech Lab — a veteran-led builder rooted in Louisiana. Square Bidness represents everyday people who handle business with pride and professionalism, blending limited-run apparel with real field execution and documented operations.

The Tech Lab side exists to systematize the work: standards, checklists, repeatable installs, clean documentation, and expansion readiness — so partners, lenders, and customers can see the proof.

What Square Bidness stands on

  • Principles first — quality over shortcuts.
  • Morals — protect the name and the customer.
  • Business done right — documented work, clean finish, and consistency.
