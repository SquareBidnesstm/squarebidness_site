Lender-facing overview

Marcus Buckner is the founder and principal operator of Square Bidness, a Louisiana-based operating company managing apparel operations, physical facilities, and emerging commerce and AI infrastructure. The company applies standardized internal processes to support operational consistency, scope control, and long-term asset viability.

Operational responsibility

Marcus maintains direct responsibility for:

  • Oversight of facilities (HQ1, HQ2)
  • Vendor selection, coordination, and scope control
  • Capital deployment tied to equipment and build-outs
  • Internal oversight of critical workstreams in apparel, infrastructure, and tech
  • Documentation and verification of completed work

Risk management

Risk is managed through incremental phases, controlled vendor engagement, internal execution standards, and documentation at each milestone. Expansion is approached with a measured timeline and an emphasis on asset use value — headquarters, slab, and infrastructure — rather than speculative growth.

Internal methodology: SB Install Method

The SB Install Method is an internal operational framework used to guide planning, installation, verification, and documentation for physical and technical environments — from HQ build-outs to lab and infrastructure upgrades.

  • Pre-install planning and budgeting
  • Step-based execution
  • Field validation
  • Documentation for maintenance and replication
