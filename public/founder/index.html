Founder

Marcus Buckner

Founder of Square Bidness Apparel & Square Bidness Tech Lab

Marcus Buckner is a veteran founder and builder based in Louisiana, leading Square Bidness Apparel and Square Bidness Tech Lab — a brand and infrastructure platform rooted in discipline, real-world execution, and long-term vision.

What began as apparel built for everyday people who handle business correctly has grown into a multi-site operation spanning headquarters, field labs, and documented contractor-grade work. Marcus focuses on doing things the right way: clean systems, real assets, and accountable growth.

His work blends brand, infrastructure, and technology — ensuring Square Bidness operates with the standards of a much larger organization while staying grounded in community and principle.
