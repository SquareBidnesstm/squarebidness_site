Positioning

Your pitch

Use this to introduce yourself clean and fast.

30-second pitch

I’m Marcus, founder of Square Bidness. We build digital infrastructure for independent operators — restaurants and service businesses already moving, but losing money in chaos. We don’t replace their business, we organize their demand through ordering, payments, routing, and cleaner operations. We already have live systems running in Louisiana with real orders and real revenue.

Short version

I build systems for real businesses so they can organize demand, take payments clean, and operate with less chaos.

Execution

Room strategy

Move like a builder with purpose, not like a tourist.

Who to target

  • Founders building real things
  • Investors and ecosystem connectors
  • Operators who understand messy real-world businesses

What to avoid

  • Idea-only conversations that go nowhere
  • People with no real traction
  • Long conversations with no signal

Where to stand

  • Near coffee
  • Near transitions between sessions
  • Near entry and exit paths
