Where It Started

These early custom memorial pieces from 2013 helped define the tribute lane Philson Le Fleuriste is known for today. The Dream Chaser DC and RNIC builds were among the first original statement pieces that pushed the work beyond standard funeral flowers into true memorial art.

Origin story: These pieces trace back to the early Cane River Funeral Home era and represent some of the first custom tribute builds that helped shape Eddie Philson’s memorial style.
