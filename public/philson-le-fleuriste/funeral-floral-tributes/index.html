What Philson builds

Tribute florals are built around the person being honored — not pulled from a generic catalog.

Name Lettering + Messages

Names, initials, dates, and messages such as “Forever Loved” built with care and service-room presence.

Career + Lifestyle Tributes

Truck drivers, barbers, musicians, fashion, hobbies, businesses, and symbols that reflect real life.

Military, agencies, organizations, service groups, and honor pieces for ceremonies that require respect and finish.
