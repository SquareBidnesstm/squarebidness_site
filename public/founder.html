Marcus Buckner — Founder of Square Bidness

Principles. Morals. Business Done Right.

The Story

Marcus Buckner is the founder of Square Bidness Apparel and the creator of SB Tech Lab, a dual-lane brand that blends lifestyle, fashion, construction discipline, and emerging technology from the ground up.

Born and raised in Louisiana, Marcus built Square Bidness as a tribute to everyday people who handle business with integrity — no shortcuts, no excuses, and always with pride. From T-shirts to limited-run collections, from recessed lighting installs to data-center prep work, Marcus has shaped Square Bidness into a brand rooted in authenticity, skill, and execution.

Brand Leader & Builder

Over the last few years, Marcus expanded the brand from apparel into multi-site physical infrastructure including:

  • HQ1 — Hammond headquarters & production center
  • HQ2 — Pineville electrical & field lab
  • 40×50 slab — future home of SB Tech Lab Phase II

This hands-on work is the backbone of the Square Bidness Install Method — a field philosophy built on precision, consistency, and pride.

Media Recognition

Marcus has been featured by Marquis Who’s Who and is actively expanding Square Bidness into national visibility, including opportunities with strategist Heather Villani and billboard placements.

What’s Next

Square Bidness enters 2026 with apparel growth, contractor capability, and a technology roadmap that connects Louisiana communities with future-facing infrastructure.

This is just the beginning.
