Who we are

Square Bidness Apparel started as a simple idea — make clothes for the people who really move the needle. The ones handling business, raising families, serving communities, and keeping their name solid wherever they go.

Founded by a U.S. veteran and rooted in Louisiana, the brand has grown from early T-shirt drops into full collections like VSOP, Winter Games, and Supima Essentials, with international reach and local love at the same time.

How we move

We build on a simple promise: if it carries the Square Bidness name, it needs to feel good, look right, and stand up to real life. That shows up in our fabric choices, our stitching, our photography, and the way we treat customers and partners.

  • Quality over quantity
  • American-first mindset
  • Local partners where possible
  • Long-term relationships over quick plays

Where we’re based
