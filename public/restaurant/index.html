Online ordering built around your real operation.

Square Bidness installs ordering systems for restaurants that need cleaner pickup, fewer missed calls, and a more organized catering flow.

  • QR menu and ordering page
  • Prepaid pickup checkout
  • Live order screen for staff
  • Catering request and deposit flow
  • Operator controls for menu changes

QR Menu

Customers scan at the counter, table, event, or flyer and land on the restaurant order page.

Prepaid Pickup

Customers pay before the order hits your kitchen, which keeps pickup cleaner and reduces fake orders.

Live Orders

Staff can see incoming paid orders, customer details, notes, and order status in one place.

Operator Controls

Pause ordering, mark sold out, update item notes, and adjust menu flow without rebuilding the site.

Catering Flow

Collect event requests, guest count, service needs, and follow-up details without clogging pickup orders.
