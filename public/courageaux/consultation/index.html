Who this is for

New clients start here.

If you’re booking your first visit, starting corrective skin care, or you just want clarity before you come in — a consultation keeps everything clean, calm, and professional.

  • First-time clients (or first time in a while).
  • Clients moving into corrective or advanced treatments.
  • Anyone who wants expectations before booking or walking in.

Online booking, reminders, intake forms, and waivers are handled securely through GlossGenius.

What happens

Clear plan, realistic goals.

  • Review your skin history, sensitivities, and current products.
  • Discuss top concerns: acne, hyperpigmentation, texture, barrier support, etc.
  • Match you to the right service level — not guesswork.
  • Set a routine-first plan for home + in-studio consistency.

Many new clients start with the “Hard Reset” first-time facial or a consultation.

Before you arrive

Best results start before the chair.

  • Come with a clean face when possible (no heavy makeup).
  • Bring a list/photos of your current skin products if you can.
  • Include medications, recent peels, or procedures in your intake.
  • Arrive a few minutes early to settle in.

For walk-ins: availability depends on the day’s schedule. If you want a guaranteed time, book online.
