Who this is for

If you’re new, start here.

Whether you’re booking your very first facial, coming in for corrective skin care, or adding waxing and dermaplaning — a clear consultation keeps everything calm and professional.

  • New clients who’ve never visited Courageaux Aesthetics.
  • Existing clients switching into corrective or advanced treatments.
  • Anyone who wants to understand expectations before booking.

Online booking, reminders, intake forms, and waivers are handled securely through GlossGenius.

What happens

Clean conversation before clean skin.

  • Review your skin history, current products, and lifestyle.
  • Talk through top concerns: acne, dark marks, texture, sensitivity, etc.
  • Set realistic goals and a plan — both in-studio and at home.
  • Recommend the right facial or treatment level for your skin barrier.

Most new clients either book a virtual consultation or the “Hard Reset” first-time facial through the services menu.

Before your appointment

Help your skin — and your esthetician.

  • Arrive with a bare face when possible (no heavy makeup or strip lashes).
  • Bring a list or photos of your current skin products if you can.
  • Mention any medications, peels, or recent procedures in your intake form.
  • Give yourself a few extra minutes to park, breathe, and settle in.

You’ll complete any required intake forms and waivers digitally via GlossGenius so everything is documented and protected.

Policies

Respecting your time & the schedule.

The full, always-up-to-date policies — deposits, reschedules, no-shows, late arrivals — live on the official booking site.

  • All bookings and changes are managed through GlossGenius.
  • Deposits (if required) are processed through the booking system.
  • Late arrivals may shorten your service so the next client starts on time.
  • No-shows and last-minute changes can result in fees per the booking policy.

For the most accurate details, review the policy section while booking on courageauxaesthetics.glossgenius.com.
