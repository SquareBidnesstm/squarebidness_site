About Monica

Monica designs bold, refined residential spaces with a luxury finish. Her signature blends warm gold accents, clean lines, sculptural pieces, and layered textures to create rooms that feel elevated — and still lived-in.

Every project is curated with intention: structure + softness, statement + comfort, and a final look that photographs beautifully while functioning day-to-day.
