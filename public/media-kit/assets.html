Typography

Use system fonts for speed and load time. For editorial layouts, match clean sans-serif styling.

  • Headlines: -apple-system, system-ui, Segoe UI, sans-serif
  • Body: system-ui, Segoe UI, Roboto, Arial
  • Weight: 500–700 for headlines, 400–500 for body

Do not distort, stretch, or outline the Square Bidness logos. Keep at least one “S” width of clear space around the logo in all directions.

Usage Guidelines

  • Do not recolor the logos outside the approved palette without written approval.
  • Do not place the logo over busy backgrounds that hurt legibility.
  • Do not rotate, skew, or place effects (glow, drop shadow) directly on the marks.
  • For apparel mockups, keep proportions true — no stretched or warped logos.
  • When in doubt, use the main wordmark on black or white for press.
