Founder — Marcus Buckner

Marcus Buckner is the founder of Square Bidness Apparel and SB Tech Lab, a Louisiana-rooted brand that treats lifestyle, infrastructure, and technology as one lane.

From wiring recessed lights and mapping breakers at HQ1 and HQ2, to building digital infrastructure and lender-ready documentation, Marcus operates like a contractor and a founder at the same time. He built Square Bidness to prove that a local brand can own its story, its property, and its technology stack — not just its logo.

His core code: “Principles. Morals. Business Done Right.”
