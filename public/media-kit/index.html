Founder & Brand Story

About the Founder — Marcus Buckner

Marcus Buckner is the founder of Square Bidness Apparel and the Square Bidness Tech Lab, a veteran-led brand and infrastructure project rooted in Louisiana.

What started as limited t-shirt runs for everyday professionals has grown into a multi-site operation with:

  • HQ1 in Hammond — primary headquarters and production hub
  • HQ2 in Pineville — active field lab and secondary office
  • A 40×50 slab behind HQ1 reserved for future data and fabrication buildout

Marcus documents his own electrical, construction, and buildout journey in real time, then uses that discipline to inform the way he runs his brand, supports local contractors, and prepares for national-level partnerships.

About the Brand — Square Bidness Apparel

Square Bidness Apparel is built around the idea that doing good business is a lifestyle. The brand serves professionals, field workers, and community leaders who value respect, morals, and showing up correctly in every room.

Collections like VSOP and Winter Game pair storytelling with premium materials, limited runs, and a strong connection to Louisiana culture.

Infrastructure & Locations

HQ1 — Hammond (Primary)

Headquarters · Production · Content

  • Recessed lighting, 20A equipment circuits, and interior patch/paint work.
  • Base for apparel production, content creation, and daily operations.
  • Home of the original Tech Lab building and 40×50 slab.

HQ2 — Pineville (Field Lab)

Training · Testing · Documentation

  • Used for electrical, LVP, and recessed-lighting training.
  • Shows the Square Bidness Install Method in a real house.
  • Backup base and future staging area for gear and inventory.

Start2Finish Partnership

Square Bidness works closely with Start2Finish Contracting, led by Diante “Boo Brady” Calvert, on full-house renovation projects in Louisiana.

One Alexandria “mini mansion” is fully documented through SB Tech Lab — from outlets and switches to canless recessed lighting and plates — giving lenders and media a verified look at real work completed as a team.
