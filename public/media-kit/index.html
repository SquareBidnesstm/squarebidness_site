Founder & Brand Story

About the Founder — Marcus Buckner

Marcus Buckner is the founder of Square Bidness Apparel and Square Bidness Tech Lab — a veteran-led owner-operator building an apparel business and an infrastructure lane rooted in Louisiana.

About the Brand — Square Bidness Apparel

Square Bidness Apparel is a Louisiana-based apparel brand founded on January 4, 2022. The company releases limited-run products across casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear categories — emphasizing clean presentation, durability, and real-world use.

About the Tech Lab — Infrastructure Behind the Brand

Square Bidness Tech Lab is the internal build-and-document lane behind the business — supporting operations, internal tools (including Tax Pass™), and a phased approach to future infrastructure buildouts.

Visit lab.squarebidness.com Tech Labs (brand page) Tax Pass™

Infrastructure & Footprint

Square Bidness operates a multi-site footprint in Louisiana, supported by company-owned land, facilities, and a phased infrastructure strategy through the Square Bidness Tech Lab.

HQ1 — Hammond (Primary Headquarters)

Operations · Production · Planning

  • Approximately 1.07 acres owned by the company
  • Daily operations, production, and content hub
  • 40×50 (approx. 2,000 sq ft) concrete slab reserved for Phase II Tech Lab expansion

HQ2 — Pineville (Field Lab)

Training · Testing · Buildout Documentation

  • Secondary headquarters and active field lab
  • Hands-on electrical, flooring, lighting, and renovation work
  • Used to document real-world execution through the Tech Lab

Phased Tech Lab Development

Phase I — Foundation & Documentation (Active)

Documented field work, hands-on builds, and internal operating systems. HQ2 supports active testing and execution documentation.

Phase II — Dedicated Lab Space (Site-Ready)

HQ1 slab is in place for modular lab buildout, equipment staging, and future infrastructure systems.

Phase III — Asset Utilization & Scale (Planned)

Scale via additional company-owned assets as demand and capital allow, while maintaining ownership and discipline.

This phased approach is designed to expand responsibly while maintaining control, transparency, and long-term ownership.
