Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bee's Angels LLC d/b/a Square Bidness Health ("Square Bidness Health," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information submitted through health.squarebidness.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting our CNA float pool application or contacting us, including:

  • Full name and phone number
  • Email address and city
  • CNA certification number and expiration date
  • Work availability and preferences
  • SMS consent status

SMS / Text Messaging

We do not share, sell, or rent your mobile phone number to any third party for marketing purposes.

By providing your phone number and selecting "Yes" to SMS consent on the CNA application form, you agree to receive text messages from Square Bidness Health at (225) 209-9257 regarding:

  • Application status updates
  • Shift availability and placement notifications
  • Certification reminders
  • General operational communications

Message frequency: Low volume. Messages are sent infrequently and only as relevant to your application or placement status.

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your carrier for details.

To opt out: Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process and manage your CNA float pool application
  • Contact you regarding shift availability and placements
  • Send SMS notifications if you have opted in
  • Comply with applicable healthcare staffing regulations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Healthcare facilities where you are placed, as necessary for the placement
  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., Supabase for data storage, Twilio for SMS delivery) under confidentiality obligations
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Application data is stored securely and accessible only to authorized Square Bidness Health personnel.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at the information below.
