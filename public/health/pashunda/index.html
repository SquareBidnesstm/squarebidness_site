Insurance Coverage — What Each Type Covers
General Liability (GL)

Covers third-party claims for bodily injury or property damage connected to business operations — for example, if someone is injured during a facility visit or other business-related activity. Baseline coverage every operating business carries.

Professional Liability (PL)

Also called Errors & Omissions. Covers claims of negligence or failure to properly perform professional duties. For a staffing agency, this is what protects clinical oversight, CNA placement decisions, and supervisory work if a claim is made against the business's professional judgment.

Workers' Compensation

Covers medical costs and lost wages for employees injured on the job. Required once CNAs are hired as W-2 staff. RN partners are LLC members, not employees, so this primarily applies to the CNA workforce once placements begin.
