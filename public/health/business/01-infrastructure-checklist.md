# Square Bidness Health — Business Infrastructure Checklist

## PHASE 1: LLC Foundation (Do These First)

### Louisiana LLC — Confirm Status
- [ ] Louisiana Secretary of State: confirm LLC is in good standing
      → sos.la.gov/BusinessServices/SearchBusinessFiling → search "Square Bidness Health"
- [ ] EIN (Employer Identification Number) confirmed active with IRS
      → Call IRS Business & Specialty Tax Line: 800-829-4933
- [ ] Separate business bank account open (not commingled with personal funds)
- [ ] Operating Agreement in place (even single-member LLC needs this)
- [ ] Louisiana Annual Report filed (due by the anniversary of formation each year)

### Legal Compliance for Healthcare Staffing
- [ ] Louisiana Workforce Commission: verify no staffing agency license required for CNA placement
      → laworks.net → Employer Services
- [ ] Louisiana State Board of Nursing: confirm RN supervisory structure meets state requirements
      → lsbn.state.la.us
- [ ] Workers' compensation insurance in place for placed CNAs
- [ ] General liability insurance (healthcare staffing minimum: $1M per occurrence, $3M aggregate)
- [ ] Professional liability / errors & omissions coverage
- [ ] Obtain W-9 from each CNA (required for IRS 1099 if independent contractors)
      OR confirm CNAs are W-2 employees (strongly preferred for grant eligibility and RN supervision compliance)

## PHASE 2: Federal Grant Infrastructure

### SAM.gov Registration (Required Before ANY Federal Grant)
- [ ] Step 1: Create Login.gov account at login.gov (use personal email)
- [ ] Step 2: Go to sam.gov → Register New Entity
- [ ] Step 3: Entity type → "Business or Organization" → "For-Profit Organization"
- [ ] Step 4: Enter EIN exactly as it appears on your SS-4 / IRS confirmation letter
- [ ] Step 5: Enter legal business name exactly as registered with Louisiana SOS
- [ ] Step 6: Physical address (cannot be PO Box for primary)
- [ ] Step 7: NAICS codes:
      PRIMARY:   561320 — Temporary Help Services
      SECONDARY: 621610 — Home Health Care Services
      SECONDARY: 624120 — Services for Elderly and Persons with Disabilities
- [ ] Step 8: Enter bank account info for EFT payments (routing + account number)
- [ ] Step 9: Submit → wait 4–6 weeks for validation and active UEI assignment
- [ ] Step 10: Set annual renewal reminder (expires 365 days from activation)
- [ ] Once active: register on grants.gov using your UEI

### HPSA Designation Confirmation
Pull the official HPSA IDs for all four service parishes. You need these numbers cited in federal grant applications.
- [ ] Go to data.hrsa.gov/tools/shortage-area/hpsa-find
- [ ] Search each parish:
      Rapides Parish, Louisiana
      Tangipahoa Parish, Louisiana
      Washington Parish, Louisiana
      St. Helena Parish, Louisiana
- [ ] Record HPSA ID and score for each (score 1–25; higher = more underserved = higher grant priority)
- [ ] Save HPSA designation certificates — grant reviewers want documentation

### Louisiana State Vendor Registration
Required for any LDH, LED, or state agency grants.
- [ ] Register at lacontractorregistration.la.gov (general contractor registration)
- [ ] Register on Louisiana's LaGov vendor portal: wwwcfg.doa.la.gov/osp
- [ ] Request Certificate of Good Standing from Louisiana SOS (often required for state grants, $10 fee)

## PHASE 3: Nonprofit Arm Formation (See 02-nonprofit-formation-guide.md)

### Square Bidness Health Foundation, Inc.
- [ ] Decide on registered agent (can be owner initially)
- [ ] Recruit at least 3 board members (required for Louisiana nonprofit)
- [ ] File Articles of Incorporation with Louisiana Secretary of State ($75)
      → Use draft in 02-nonprofit-formation-guide.md
- [ ] Apply for nonprofit EIN (separate from LLC EIN) at irs.gov/businesses/small/article/0,,id=102767,00.html
- [ ] Draft bylaws (template in 02-nonprofit-formation-guide.md)
- [ ] Hold organizational board meeting, adopt bylaws, elect officers
- [ ] Open separate nonprofit bank account
- [ ] File IRS Form 1023-EZ (if projected receipts < $50K/year) or 1023 (full)
      → Fee: $275 (1023-EZ) or $600 (1023)
      → Processing: 3–6 months for 1023-EZ, 6–12 months for full 1023
- [ ] Register nonprofit on SAM.gov (separate UEI from LLC)
- [ ] Register on Candid/GuideStar (free nonprofit profile — grant funders check this)

## PHASE 4: Operational Documentation for Grant Applications

### What Grant Reviewers Want to See
- [ ] Organizational chart showing:
      → Owner / CEO
      → RN #1 (clinical oversight, supervisory visits, care plan development)
      → RN #2 (clinical oversight, QA, Medicare compliance)
      → CNA pool members
      → Facility clients
- [ ] Service area map showing Rapides, Tangipahoa, Washington, St. Helena parishes
- [ ] Client roster (even 2–3 facility relationships = proof of operations)
- [ ] Sample care oversight documentation (how RNs supervise CNA placements)
- [ ] 2 years financial records (or from inception if < 2 years old)
- [ ] Letters of support from:
      → At least 2 facilities you serve or have served
      → A parish health department or clinic in your service area
      → A community organization in the parishes

## PHASE 5: Immediate Revenue + Cost Offset

### Louisiana IWTP / SBET — Apply Now
- [ ] Go to laworks.net/WorkforceDev/IWTP
- [ ] Submit IWTP application (employer-driven, no competitive cycle)
- [ ] List each CNA you will train
- [ ] Identify training: IV certification, medication aide, specialty skills, safety courses
- [ ] Reimbursement: up to $3,000 per CNA per fiscal year
- [ ] Louisiana fiscal year: July 1 – June 30
