## GRANT APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST

Things to have ready BEFORE submitting any grant application:

**Organization**
- [ ] EIN (LLC + Foundation if applicable)
- [ ] SAM.gov UEI (active, not expired)
- [ ] Louisiana SOS Certificate of Good Standing
- [ ] Organizational chart (owner → RNs → CNA pool → facilities)
- [ ] NAICS codes registered on SAM.gov

**Financials**
- [ ] 2 years of financial records (or from inception)
- [ ] Business bank statements (3 months minimum)
- [ ] Budget narrative for grant funds requested

**Clinical / Operational**
- [ ] RN license numbers and Louisiana Board of Nursing verification
- [ ] Description of clinical oversight model (how RNs supervise CNA placements)
- [ ] CNA certification verification process
- [ ] Service area description with HPSA IDs for each parish

**Community Need**
- [ ] HPSA designation certificates (data.hrsa.gov/tools/shortage-area/hpsa-find)
- [ ] Data on CNA workforce shortage in your service area (LDH parish health profiles)
- [ ] Letters of support (2+ facilities + 1 community organization minimum)

**Narrative Hooks (use these in every application)**
- Rural Louisiana, HPSA-designated parishes
- 2 licensed RNs providing clinical oversight
- 3-node hardened infrastructure — operational during weather emergencies
- CNA workforce shortage directly limiting access to post-acute care
- Louisiana-owned and operated
