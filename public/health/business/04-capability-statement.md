# Square Bidness Health, LLC
## Capability Statement

---

**CORE COMPETENCIES**

Square Bidness Health is a Louisiana-based licensed CNA staffing and rural health workforce company providing certified nursing assistant placement services under registered nurse clinical oversight across rural Louisiana. Our services address the critical shortage of verified, supervised CNA talent in medically underserved communities.

- **CNA Float Pool Management** — maintaining a pool of verified, credentialed CNAs available for short-notice and scheduled placements at rural facilities
- **RN Clinical Supervision** — all CNA placements supervised by on-staff registered nurses; includes supervisory visits, care plan review, and Medicare compliance documentation
- **Rural Healthcare Staffing** — specialization in parishes designated as Health Professional Shortage Areas (HPSAs) and Medically Underserved Areas (MUAs)
- **Workforce Pipeline Development** — recruiting, credentialing, and training CNAs for long-term care, home health, rural health clinic, and critical access hospital settings
- **Hardened Operations Infrastructure** — 3-node facility network with independent power across central, southeast, and north Louisiana, ensuring continuity of operations during weather emergencies

---

**DIFFERENTIATORS**

- **RN-supervised model** — every CNA placement carries registered nurse oversight, elevating care quality and regulatory compliance beyond standard staffing agency models
- **Rural-first focus** — operations centered in HPSA-designated parishes where workforce shortage is most acute
- **Louisiana-owned and operated** — deep local knowledge, community relationships, and vested interest in long-term regional health outcomes
- **Infrastructure resilience** — hardened 3-node grid (Hammond HQ1 operational, Pineville HQ2 in build, Mt. Herman Archive staged) provides business continuity when cloud-dependent competitors go offline
- **Rapid deployment capability** — float pool model designed for short-notice fill requests, critical in rural facilities with no local backup staffing resources
