# Square Bidness Health Foundation, Inc.
## Louisiana Nonprofit Corporation — Formation Guide

---

## Why Form This Nonprofit

The LLC handles operations. The Foundation handles:
- Federal grant applications (USDA, LCDBG, DRA SEDAP — nonprofits only)
- CNA training pipeline partnerships with community colleges (H.E.R.O. Fund lead partner)
- Community health workforce development programs
- 501(c)(3) status = donors can deduct contributions (unlocks philanthropic funding)

**The Foundation does not compete with the LLC.** It feeds it — trained CNAs flow from the Foundation's pipeline into the LLC's float pool.

## Louisiana Filing Steps

**Fee: $75**
**Where: Louisiana Secretary of State**
**Online: geauxbiz.sos.la.gov**

1. Go to geauxbiz.sos.la.gov
2. Select "Form a New Business" → "Nonprofit Corporation"
3. Enter the proposed name: **Square Bidness Health Foundation, Inc.**
4. Confirm name availability (search first at sos.la.gov)
5. Enter registered agent name and street address (you, initially)
6. List initial directors (minimum 3 required in Louisiana)
7. Enter principal office address
8. Upload completed Articles of Incorporation (use draft below)
9. Pay $75 filing fee
10. Certificate of incorporation issued within 3–5 business days

## IRS 501(c)(3) Application

**After Louisiana incorporation is complete:**

1. Obtain a separate EIN for the Foundation at irs.gov → "Apply for Employer ID Number"
2. File **IRS Form 1023-EZ** (streamlined) if:
   - Projected gross receipts under $50,000/year for next 3 years, AND
   - Total assets under $250,000
   - Fee: $275
   - Processing: typically 2–4 weeks (vs. 6–12 months for full 1023)
3. File **IRS Form 1023** (full) if:
   - Projected receipts exceed $50,000/year (likely once grants come in)
   - Fee: $600
   - More thorough but reviewers have less discretion to deny

**Recommendation:** Start with 1023-EZ. You can always amend. Get the status established quickly so you can apply for H.E.R.O. Fund Round 3 as a partner organization.

## Relationship Structure: LLC + Foundation

```
Square Bidness Health, LLC (Operations)
  ↕ affiliated entity
Square Bidness Health Foundation, Inc. (Workforce Development + Grants)

Foundation activities:
  - CNA training pipeline
  - Community college partnerships
  - Grant applications (H.E.R.O., DRA, USDA CF, LCDBG)
  - Community outreach and workforce research

LLC activities:
  - CNA float pool operations
  - Facility contracts and placements
  - RN clinical supervision
  - Revenue generation from staffing fees

How they connect:
  - Foundation graduates trained CNAs → LLC recruits from that pipeline
  - Foundation receives training grants → funds CNA education costs
  - LLC provides clinical placement sites and mentors for Foundation programs
  - Shared brand identity: Square Bidness Health
```

**Key governance rule:** Board of the Foundation must be majority-independent (not all LLC owners/employees). At least 2 of your 3 initial directors should be community members, healthcare professionals, or organizational partners — not just the LLC owner.
