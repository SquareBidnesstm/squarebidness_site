Fabric, fit & care

How VSOP is built

Very Superior Old Pale is an aged concept — premium cloth, embroidery, and details that feel like they’ve been thought through.

  • VSOP color story: deep burgundy with brown blocking and gold-toned embroidery hits.
  • Jacket: hooded silhouette, zip front, lined for everyday wear, embroidery at chest and/or back (final placements locked in at production).
  • Shorts: above-the-knee athletic cut with comfortable waistband and embroidered mark.
  • Built for Fall behavior — from Louisiana evenings to airport runs and NYC sidewalks.
  • Care: cold wash inside out, hang dry to protect embroidery and color depth.

Concept & motion

What VSOP really stands for

VSOP stands for Very Superior Old Pale — a term from aged spirits. For Square Bidness, it’s about work that’s been aging quietly in the background, building value over time.

This set represents that same energy: grown, select, not loud for no reason. Burgundy and brown, stitched up with embroidery and intention — more cigar lounge and rooftop talk than random tee off the rack.

From Hammond to New Orleans, up to New York and back home, VSOP is built for people who carry themselves with principle and still like to step out clean when it’s time.
