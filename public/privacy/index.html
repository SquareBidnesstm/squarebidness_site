Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 29, 2025

At Square Bidness Apparel (“Square Bidness”, “we”, “us”, “our”), your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account & Order Info: name, email, shipping/billing address, phone (if provided).
  • Payment: processed securely by our payment partner (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage Data: device/browser info, pages viewed, referring URLs, and analytics/cookie events to improve the site.
  • Support: messages you send us (email, forms), and attachments you choose to share.
  • Marketing Opt-ins: email address and preferences when you subscribe (e.g., via Mailchimp).

2) How We Use Information

  • Process and deliver orders; provide customer support.
  • Operate, maintain, personalize, and improve our website and products.
  • Send updates and promotions if you opt in (unsubscribe anytime).
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations and our Terms.

3) Payments

Transactions are handled by our payment processor (e.g., Stripe). They receive the payment information needed to process your order and are responsible for their own handling of that data. We retain only limited transaction metadata (e.g., last 4 digits, brand) for receipts and support.

4) Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand traffic and improve the experience. You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Learn more at Google Policies.

5) Email Marketing (Mailchimp)

If you subscribe, your email and preference data are processed by our email provider (Mailchimp). Every message includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email us to be removed.

6) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted vendors as needed to run our business:

  • Payments (e.g., Stripe)
  • Fulfillment & shipping partners (for delivery)
  • Email/analytics providers (opt-in marketing and site performance)
  • Fraud prevention and security services
  • Professional services (e.g., accountants) where appropriate

7) Data Retention

  • Orders: generally kept for 7 years for tax, accounting, and warranty purposes.
  • Marketing: kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Support: kept as long as needed to resolve your request and maintain proper records.

8) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your data. You can:

  • Unsubscribe from marketing via the link in emails.
  • Request access/deletion by emailing us.
  • Adjust cookie settings in your browser.

We will verify your request and respond within the time required by applicable law.

9) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (including HTTPS/SSL). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10) Children’s Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11) International Transfers

We are US-based. If you access our site from outside the US, your information may be processed in the US or other countries with different data protection laws.

12) Third-Party Links

We may link to third-party sites (e.g., Instagram, Stripe, Mailchimp). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

13) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we change the “Last updated” date above. Material changes may be announced on the site.
