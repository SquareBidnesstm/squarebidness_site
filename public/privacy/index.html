Privacy Policy

We respect your time, money, and information. This page outlines in plain language how we use and protect the information connected to your orders and your visits to this site.

1. What we collect

When you shop with Square Bidness or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact info (name, email, shipping address).
  • Order details and transaction information.
  • Basic site analytics (page views, traffic sources, approximate location).

2. How we use your information

We use this information to:

  • Process and ship your orders.
  • Communicate about your order status or customer support requests.
  • Understand what is resonating so we can improve products, site performance, and content.

We do not sell your personal information. We may use trusted third-party tools (like payment processors and analytics) to operate the store.

3. Payments

Payments are handled by secure third-party processors (such as Stripe or similar services). We do not store your full card details on our own servers.

4. Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies, tags, and analytics scripts to better understand how visitors interact with the site — for example, which pages are visited and from what general region.

These tools help us prioritize what to build next and keep the experience smooth and fast.

5. Security

The site and infrastructure are built and maintained by Square Bidness Tech Lab. We use standard security practices (such as HTTPS) to help protect your information in transit. No system is perfect, but we take our responsibility seriously.

6. Your choices

If you’d like to update or correct your contact details, or if you have questions about how your information is used, email us at squarebidnessapparel@gmail.com.
