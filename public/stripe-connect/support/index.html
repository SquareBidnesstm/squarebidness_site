Let's Get You Connected.

Follow these steps in order. Takes about 5 minutes.

  1. 1
    Click "Connect to Stripe Now" below. This takes you to the connection page.
  2. 2
    Check the box on that page. It says "My business is ready to connect to Stripe." Check it.
  3. 3
    Click the gold button. Stripe will open. Fill out everything they ask — name, business info, bank account.
  4. 4
    Submit and come back. Once you finish in Stripe, you'll be sent back here automatically.
