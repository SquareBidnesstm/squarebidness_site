Care, Fabric & Motion

A quick rundown so your Supima keeps doing its job all year.

What makes Supima different?

Supima uses extra-long staple cotton — smoother hand, better color retention, and less pilling than regular tees. It feels good on day one and still looks right after real wear.

How should I wash it?

Cold wash, inside-out, low heat dry or hang dry if you can. That keeps the print, neck, and color moving how they’re supposed to.

Where does this fit in the Square Bidness lineup?

VSOP is the aged, premium lane. Winter Games is the outerwear and hat play. Supima is the everyday uniform that ties it all together — from Hammond errands to New York trips.
