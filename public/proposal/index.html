Dear Lender,

Square Bidness Apparel, LLC is expanding into a dual-purpose operation — premium apparel manufacturing and cutting-edge local technology infrastructure — to support long-term growth, job creation, and stability for the Hammond region. Our company owns the building and land at 46331 N Baptist Rd, Hammond, LA 70401, which serves as our base of operations and the future home of the Square Bidness Tech Lab: an on-site production space, data center hub, and community-driven business accelerator.

We are seeking $150,000 in funding to complete renovations, acquire commercial-grade manufacturing equipment, and build the initial phase of our tech infrastructure. This expansion positions Square Bidness as a local economic engine — increasing production capacity, enabling contract embroidery services, creating regional distribution, and laying the foundation for scalable technology operations.

Our business is veteran-led, locally rooted, and already fully operational. With strong community ties, proven demand, and a 2.5-year track record, this proposal reflects a strategic investment into a company that is built to last, built on land we own, and built with a clear growth pathway.

This funding will support the next stage of Square Bidness: an American-made, Louisiana-grown brand evolving into a modern manufacturing and tech enterprise.
