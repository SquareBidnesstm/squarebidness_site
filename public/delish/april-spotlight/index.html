How to use the system

Simple online pickup ordering.

Choose your main plate, select your 2 sides, add extras if needed, choose your pickup window, and pay online before arrival.

1. Choose your main plate

Select the day’s available main plate and quantity. Each main plate requires side selections before checkout.

2. Select your 2 sides

Main plates require 2 side selections. That helps Delish receive cleaner, more complete pickup orders.

3. Add extras if needed

Add a drink, an extra side, or a Lagniappe item before choosing your pickup time window and paying online.
