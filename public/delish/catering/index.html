How it works

Simple intake. Clear follow-up. Less confusion.

The catering process is simple: submit your request, wait for review, secure your approved date with a deposit, and finalize the details directly with Delish.

1

Customer submits request

They send event basics, menu ideas, timing, and contact info in one clean form.

2

Delish reviews details

Date, headcount, availability, and requested items are checked before anything is promised.

3

Deposit secures the date

Approved catering orders require a 25% non-refundable deposit before the date is locked in.

4

Final details get confirmed

Delish confirms final menu, pickup or delivery details, and remaining payment timing directly with the customer.
