Privacy Policy

Delish
Effective Date: March 25, 2026

Information We Collect

Delish may collect a customer’s name, phone number, email address, pickup details, catering request details, and order information when voluntarily provided during checkout or catering request submission.

How We Use Information

Customer information is used exclusively to process food orders, manage pickup scheduling, respond to catering requests, send transactional updates, and confirm approved catering deposits.

Transactional SMS Use Only

Phone numbers are used only for transactional communications related to active food orders or catering requests. These may include order confirmations, pickup notifications, catering status updates, and deposit/payment confirmations.

No Marketing Use

Delish does not use customer phone numbers for marketing or promotional SMS campaigns through this program.

Information Sharing

Customer information, including phone numbers and email addresses, is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or promotional use.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect customer information and limit access to authorized systems only.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Delish directly.
