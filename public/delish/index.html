Catering

Catering requests, organized the right way.

Large orders should not get lost in the same flow as daily plates. Delish catering requests will be handled in a cleaner, separate lane with event details, review, and follow-up.

What catering requests should include

  • Event date
  • Pickup or delivery
  • Guest count
  • Budget range
  • Desired menu items
  • Best callback number

Delish catering policy

Catering requests are not confirmed until reviewed by Delish. A deposit may be required to secure your date.
