Online ordering lane

Prepaid online pickup is being put in place.

Delish is moving to a cleaner ordering system built around prepaid online pickup. No call-in orders. Daily availability and live item access will be managed through the online order lane as the system is put in place.

No call-in orders

Delish is not taking call-in orders. The order system is being built to support prepaid online pickup only, with cleaner order handling, controlled availability, and less confusion during busy periods.

Current status:
The prepaid online ordering system is being put in place now. Featured items, pickup timing, and checkout flow will be handled through the online order lane as it goes live.

What this system is being built to do

  • Accept prepaid online pickup orders only
  • Remove confusion from phone-based ordering
  • Control what items are available each day
  • Support cleaner pickup timing and confirmation
