Returns & Exchanges

We build every piece with intention. If something isn’t right, we’ll do our best to make it right within the guidelines below.

Section #1

Return window & basic requirements

Unless otherwise stated on a specific product page, we accept returns on eligible items within 30 days of delivery.

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in resellable condition.
  • All original tags and packaging should be included where applicable.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or order number) is required.
Section #2

Size exchanges

If you need a different size and we have inventory, we’ll work with you on an exchange.

  • Email support@squarebidness.com with your order number.
  • Let us know the size you received and the size you’re requesting.
  • We’ll confirm availability and next steps (including any shipping costs).
Section #3

Preorders & limited drops

Preorder and limited-run pieces (like VSOP and certain Winter Games items) are made and allocated based on commitments.

  • In many cases, preorders are final sale once production begins.
  • If there’s a defect or issue with workmanship, reach out and we’ll address it.
  • Any preorder-specific rules on the product page override the general policy here.
Section #4

How to start a return or exchange

To start a return or exchange, contact us:

  • Email: support@squarebidness.com
  • Include your name, order number, and a short note about the issue.
  • Photos help us move faster for defects or damage.

Once approved, we’ll share instructions for shipping your item back and what to expect next.

Section #5

Refund timing

When a refund is approved, it will be issued back to your original payment method.

  • Refund timing depends on your bank or card issuer (typically 3–10 business days).
  • We’ll email you once your return has been processed on our side.
