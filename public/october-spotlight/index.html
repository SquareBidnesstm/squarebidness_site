Marquesha Baham

Community Feature

Why Marquesha

Marquesha Baham refuses to coast. Freshly 18 (birthday October 11) and attending Southern University, she’s already launched real business ventures — creating and designing flyers and business cards, making your favorite party treats, and even selling hair, both wigs and bundles. Now she’s rapidly becoming one of the hottest party hosts in the 985, bringing professionalism and energy to every room.

The Outfit

She’s pictured in a one-of-one Square Bidness Apparel 2 piece denim set — clean denim with Western boots — built to match her drive.

What’s Next

Beyond the spotlight, Marquesha is working toward opening a child care facility — channeling her hustle into long-term service for families in the community.
