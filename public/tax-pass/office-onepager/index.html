Tax Pass™ — Office One-Pager

A digital intake lane for independent tax offices. Clients submit IDs, W-2s, and details from any phone. You get cleaner files, fewer no-shows, and a smoother season — without changing your tax software.

How It Works

Tax Pass sits in front of your existing tax software (Drake, TaxSlayer, ProSeries, etc.). Simple intake — clean dashboard.

1
Clients tap your link or QR

Your office gets a branded link + unique office code.

2
They send IDs, W-2s & details

Front/back of ID, W-2 photos, 1099s, basic questions — all mobile-friendly.

3
You work from your dashboard

Files arrive in a queue: New → In review → Ready → Filed.
