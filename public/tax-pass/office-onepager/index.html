How Tax Pass fits your office

Tax Pass doesn’t replace your tax software — it sits in front of it as a clean “front door.” You keep preparing returns in Drake, TaxSlayer, ProSeries, etc.

1
Clients start from your link or QR code

We give you a branded intake link and office code. You text it, print it on cards, or drop it on your socials — whatever matches how you already move.

2
They send IDs, W-2s, and details from their phone

Front/back of ID, W-2s, 1099s, basic questions, and quick notes. No app store, no login drama — just a simple secure link.

3
You work from a clean dashboard

Files drop into an office queue: New → In review → Ready to sign → Filed. You still do all calculations and e-file in the software you’re used to.

Built shoulder-to-shoulder with a real office in Alexandria, LA, so the flow makes sense on busy days — not just in a pitch deck.

What Tax Pass is not

  • Not a replacement for your tax software.
  • Not a big-box franchise portal that sits between you and your clients.
  • Not a “use our software or else” system.

Designed for independent offices

Whether you’re a single-location shop or a small multi-office operation, Tax Pass is built to feel like your system — not a generic third-party app.

  • Office-branded intake: Your logo, colors, and office name at the top.
  • Code-based routing: Each office gets a unique code (e.g. EFHO7IRV).
  • Client status view: Optional link so clients can check status without blowing up your phone.
  • Owner controls: You decide when intake is open/closed and how you’re notified.

Security & data handling

  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) end-to-end.
  • Files stored in secure cloud object storage.
  • Per-office configuration and access controls.
  • No ads, no selling client data — ever.

We’re happy to walk your office or IT person through the architecture and policies before rollout.

Pricing & rollout

For early offices, we’re keeping pricing and rollout simple:

  • Per-office or per-season pricing.
  • Pilot terms for the first season we work together.
  • Custom setup for multi-location shops.

First wave is intentionally tight so we can support every office properly.
