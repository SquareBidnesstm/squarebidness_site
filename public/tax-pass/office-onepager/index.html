What Tax Pass does

Tax Pass™ sits in front of your existing tax software. Clients use a branded link and office code to send IDs, W-2s, and details from any phone — you keep working inside Drake, TaxSlayer, ProSeries, and what you already know.

  • Clients start from your link or QR code.
  • They confirm contact info and answer a few questions.
  • They upload photos of IDs and W-2s from their phone.
  • Files drop into your office queue: New → In review → Ready to sign → Filed.
Built with a real office in Alexandria, LA The first rollout was built shoulder-to-shoulder with Tax Slayer — Jessica Porter, so the flow matches real season days, not just a whiteboard.
