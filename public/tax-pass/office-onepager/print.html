Tax Pass™ — Digital Intake for Tax Offices

A clean intake lane in front of the software you already use. Clients start their file from any phone — IDs, W-2s, and basic questions — routed straight into your office queue.

How it fits
1. Clients start from your link or QR code You get a branded intake link and office code. You can text it, print it on cards, or add it to your socials and website.
2. They send IDs, W-2s, and notes from their phone Front/back of ID, W-2s / 1099s, simple questions, and any special notes — no downloads, no portals, no extra logins.
3. Your team works from a clean queue Files land in an office queue with simple status steps (New → In review → Ready to sign → Filed). You keep preparing returns in the same tax software you use today.
First version built shoulder-to-shoulder with a real office in Alexandria, LA, focused on busy season days — not just theory.
Key points
  • No client app to download.
  • Office code routing keeps each location’s queue clean.
  • Optional client status view for fewer “what’s going on?” calls.
  • Owner controls for branding, messaging, and intake hours.
Security & data
  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) end-to-end.
  • Files stored in secure cloud object storage.
  • Per-office configuration and access controls.
  • No ads, no reselling client data — ever.
Pricing snapshot
  • Per-season or per-office pricing.
  • Pilot terms for the first season together.
  • Options for small chains / multi-office groups.
