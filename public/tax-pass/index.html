How it fits your office

Tax Pass doesn’t replace your tax software — it sits in front of it. Clients use a simple link + office code. You keep doing what works inside Drake, TaxSlayer, ProSeries, and similar tools.

1
Clients start from your link or QR code

Text it, print it on cards, or drop it on your socials — the office code routes the file correctly.

2
They send IDs, W-2s, and details from their phone

No app store, no account setup — just a clean intake flow that works on mobile.

3
You work from a clean queue

Files drop into stages: New → In review → Ready to sign → Filed (you still prepare returns in your platform).

What it is not

Tax Pass is the “front door.” It’s not tax software and it doesn’t change your business model.

  • Doesn’t change calculations or e-file inside your tax platform.
  • Doesn’t come between you and your clients.
  • Doesn’t force you into a specific tax product.
