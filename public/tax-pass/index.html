Tax Pass doesn’t replace your tax software — it sits in front of it. Clients use a simple link and office code. You keep doing what works inside Drake, TaxSlayer, ProSeries, and similar tools.

1

Security and data handling

Tax Pass is built with privacy-first handling and straightforward workflows — secure enough for serious documents, simple enough for everyday clients.

  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) end-to-end.
  • Files stored in secure cloud object storage.
  • Per-office configuration and access controls.
  • No ads, no selling client data.

We can walk through architecture and policy basics with your office (or IT) before rollout.

What Tax Pass is not

Tax Pass is the “front door.” It’s not trying to become your tax software or change your business model.

  • Doesn’t change calculations or e-file inside your tax platform.
  • Doesn’t come between you and your clients.
  • Doesn’t force you into a specific tax product.
