How Tax Pass fits your office

Tax Pass doesn’t replace your tax software — it sits in front of it. Clients use a simple link and office code. You keep doing what works inside Drake, TaxSlayer, ProSeries, etc.

1
Clients start from your link or QR code

We give you a branded intake link and office code. You text it, print it on cards, or drop it on your socials.

2
They send IDs, W-2s and details from their phone

Front/back of ID, W-2s, 1099s, basic questions, and quick notes. No app store, no login drama.

3
You work from a clean dashboard

Files drop into an office queue: New → In review → Ready to sign → Filed. You still prepare returns in your existing tax software.

We built the first version shoulder-to-shoulder with a real office in Alexandria, LA, so the flow makes sense on busy days — not just on paper.

Designed for independent offices

Whether you’re a single-location shop or a small multi-office operation, Tax Pass is built to feel like your system, not a generic portal.

Office-branded intake

Your logo, your colors, your office name. Clients see your brand at the top — not some random software company.

Code-based routing

Each office gets a unique code (like EFHO7IRV). That code makes sure every file lands in the right queue.

Client status view

Optional status link so clients can check Received / In review / Ready / Filed without blowing up your phone.

Owner controls

You choose when intake is open/closed, how you’re notified, and what messages clients see.

Security and data handling

Tax Pass is built the same way we treat our own brand — serious about privacy and data handling, but simple enough for everyday clients.

  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) end-to-end.
  • Files stored in secure cloud object storage.
  • Per-office configuration and access controls.
  • No ads, no selling client data — ever.

We’re happy to walk through our architecture and policies with your office or your IT person before you roll out.

What Tax Pass is not

We’re not trying to become your tax software or push you into a big-box franchise model. Tax Pass is the “front door” — that’s it.

  • We don’t change how you do calculations or e-file.
  • We don’t come between you and your clients.
  • We don’t force you into a specific tax platform.
