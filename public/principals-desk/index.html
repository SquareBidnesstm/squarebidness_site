Editorial follow-up

How this works

A quick guide for publishing inside The Principal’s Desk — clear, simple, and consistent.

Step 1

Write your piece

Keep it real and grounded. 400–800 words is perfect. Speak from experience, not theory. Topics can include leadership, school culture, student development, or real moments from your role.

Step 2

Send for publishing

Email your article to marcusbuckner@squarebidness.com. Include a title and any notes. We’ll format it into the editorial layout and publish it clean.

Step 3

Choose your cadence

Decide your rhythm: weekly for consistent voice, or monthly for deeper pieces. Both work — consistency is what builds the lane.

Where this goes next

The Principal’s Desk is meant to feel like a standing column — something you return to. Not noisy. Not forced. Just clear, elevated, and rooted in a voice that has lived the work. It can grow into monthly features, community reflections, school leadership essays, and perspective pieces that carry both weight and warmth.
