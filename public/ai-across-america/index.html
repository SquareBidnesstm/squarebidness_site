Say This

Your clean talking points

30-second intro:

“I’m Marcus Buckner, founder of Square Bidness. We build digital infrastructure for independent operators — restaurants, food trucks, lounges, and service businesses. We’re installing ordering systems, payment flows, booking systems, admin controls, SMS notifications, and workflow tools. My focus is helping Louisiana operators organize demand first, then layer automation and AI on top of real business activity.”
Short version:

“Square Bidness helps independent businesses move from phone calls, text messages, and manual chaos into clean digital workflows — ordering, payments, booking, customer intake, and operator controls.”
AI version:

“A lot of small businesses are not AI-ready because their operations are still unstructured. We’re building the systems that create clean workflows and usable business data first. That’s the foundation for practical AI adoption.”
Infrastructure version:

“The big companies have system integrators. Independent operators usually don’t. Square Bidness is building that local integration layer for restaurants, food trucks, lounges, and service businesses.”
Ask Smart Questions

Questions that make you sound grounded

For ecosystem leaders

  • What support exists for Louisiana founders deploying operational technology into small businesses?
  • Where do you see the biggest gap in Louisiana’s AI startup ecosystem?
  • Are there programs focused on practical AI adoption outside of large corporations?

For infrastructure people

  • How should small businesses fit into Louisiana’s AI infrastructure strategy?
  • Do you see shared compute eventually supporting local commerce and operator systems?
  • What has to exist before smaller businesses can realistically use AI?

For investors / startup people

  • What gets your attention more right now: pure AI tools or operational deployments with real usage?
  • How do you evaluate founders building in underserved or overlooked local markets?
  • What kind of proof would you want to see from a systems integration company serving independent operators?
