What is Square Bidness Apparel?
Square Bidness Apparel is a Louisiana-based apparel brand founded January 4, 2022, producing limited-run collections across casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear categories.
Who is the founder?
Marcus Buckner is the founder and owner-operator of Square Bidness Apparel and Square Bidness Tech Lab. He is a veteran-led operator who documents and performs much of the company’s electrical, construction, and infrastructure work firsthand.
Where is the company based?
Square Bidness operates in Louisiana with HQ1 in Hammond (primary headquarters on approximately 1.07 acres, including a completed 40×50 slab reserved for Phase II Tech Lab expansion), HQ2 in Pineville (secondary headquarters and active field lab), and a separate one-acre, company-owned property with an existing building being prepared as a future operational asset.
What categories does the brand operate in?
The brand spans casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear, released through limited drops rather than fast-fashion cycles.
What is the Square Bidness Tech Lab?
The Tech Lab documents real-world electrical, flooring, lighting, and infrastructure projects and serves as a training and testing environment for future modular, data-center-inspired systems.
Is Square Bidness seeking funding or partnerships?
Square Bidness selectively engages lenders and strategic partners for asset-backed growth, infrastructure expansion, and equipment acquisition. The company prioritizes long-term ownership, disciplined scaling, and transparent documentation.
Where can media find official assets?
Official assets are available at squarebidness.com/media-kit and via the Media Kit PDF.
