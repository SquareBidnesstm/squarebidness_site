4) Press FAQ

What is Square Bidness?
Square Bidness is a Louisiana-rooted brand founded in 2022 that produces limited-run apparel and builds disciplined operating systems through its internal Tech Lab.
Who is the founder?
Marcus Buckner is the founder and owner-operator of Square Bidness and Square Bidness Tech Lab (veteran-led).
Where is the company based?
Square Bidness operates in Louisiana, where the brand develops products, content, and documented workflows that support day-to-day operations.
What categories does the brand operate in?
Limited-run apparel across casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear—released through controlled drops rather than fast-fashion cycles.
What is Square Bidness Tech Lab?
Square Bidness Tech Lab is an internal workspace where the company documents operations, builds digital tools, and organizes systems that support the brand’s apparel releases and collaborations.
Is Square Bidness open to partnerships?
Square Bidness selectively collaborates with partners aligned with its values, aesthetic, and long-term vision.
Where can media find official assets?
Official assets are available at squarebidness.com/media-kit and via the Media Kit PDF.
