What is Square Bidness Apparel?
Square Bidness Apparel is a Louisiana-based apparel brand founded on January 4, 2022. The company designs and releases products across casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear categories, emphasizing clean presentation, durability, and practical wear.
Who is the founder?
Marcus Buckner is the founder and owner-operator of Square Bidness Apparel and the Square Bidness Tech Lab. He is a veteran-led operator who documents and performs much of the company’s electrical, construction, and infrastructure work firsthand.
Where is the company based?
Square Bidness operates multiple Louisiana locations: HQ1 in Hammond (primary headquarters), HQ2 in Pineville (secondary headquarters and active field lab), and a 40×50 (approx. 2,000 sq ft) concrete slab reserved for Phase II Tech Lab expansion behind HQ1.
What categories does the brand operate in?
Square Bidness Apparel spans casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear. Collections are released in limited runs rather than fast-fashion cycles.
What is the Square Bidness Tech Lab?
The Square Bidness Tech Lab documents real-world electrical, flooring, lighting, and infrastructure projects. It also functions as a training and testing environment for future modular, data-center-inspired builds and operational systems.
Is Square Bidness seeking funding or partnerships?
Square Bidness periodically engages lenders and strategic partners for asset-backed growth, infrastructure expansion, and equipment acquisition. The company prioritizes long-term ownership, disciplined scaling, and transparent documentation.
