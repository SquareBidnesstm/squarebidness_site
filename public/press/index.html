Brand facts

Square Bidness — at a glance

Square Bidness is a Louisiana-rooted apparel brand founded on January 4, 2022. The brand releases limited-run collections across casual, business-casual, tech-casual, and workwear, with a focus on professionalism, durability, and real-world use.
