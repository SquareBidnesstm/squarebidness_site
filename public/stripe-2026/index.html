Level 3

Answers that close rooms

These are the questions that test whether you are real.

How do you make money?

We charge for the install and ongoing system support — and in some cases we layer in subscription tools like FleetLog. The goal is long-term operator relationships, not one-off builds.

Is this custom every time?

No — we've standardized the system. It's repeatable installs with small adjustments depending on the operator.

Why not just use Shopify or Square?

Those tools exist, but most operators don't implement them properly. We don't just give tools — we install a working system that fits how they actually operate.

Who is your customer?

Operators already in motion — restaurants, service businesses — people with real demand but no structure.

How big can this get?

Every independent operator needs structure. We're starting with food and local services, but the system applies anywhere demand needs to be organized.

Why you?

Because we're already doing it in the real world — not testing ideas. We're installing systems where people are taking real orders and processing real payments.

Level 4

Execution + room strategy

Move like a sniper, not a tourist.

Room filter

  • If they not building or operating → exit
  • If no payment flow involved → low priority
  • If no traction → 2 min max

Tier 1 targets

  • Stripe employees
  • Real operators

Tier 2 targets

  • SaaS founders with traction

Avoid getting stuck

  • Idea-only founders
  • People just exploring
  • Low-signal conversations with no traction

Live Session Mode

  • What are they REALLY saying?
  • Where does this fit in my system? (checkout / payouts / automation / pricing)
  • Write ONE line: "We need to ______ at Square Bidness."

Room positioning

  • Near coffee
  • Near transitions
  • Near entrances
  • 10–15 minutes per convo, then move

Post-session move

  • Turn to ONE person — not the room
  • Ask: "What are you building right now?"
  • Then: "We're organizing demand for real-world operators."
  • Ask: "How are you handling payments today?"

Decision-maker check

Ask: Are you building it or running it?
