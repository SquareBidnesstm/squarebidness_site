Clarity

What SB Funding Prep is — and what it isn’t.

This lane exists so you don’t walk into a lender with a story that’s only halfway told. We stay honest about our role so everything stays clean for you and the institutions you work with.

What it is

Operator-built funding preparation

We help you tighten the things lenders care about: infrastructure, documentation, and a believable plan for what the capital will do in your lane.

  • Infrastructure and systems tuned for real revenue
  • Lender-friendly one-pager and packet assembly
  • Guidance rooted in lived operator experience
What it isn’t

A guarantee or a shortcut

We are not a bank, not a credit repair shop, and not a guarantee of approval. We don’t control underwriting. We help you show up ready so your work is clear on paper.

  • No promise of approval or specific terms
  • No gaming of income, documents, or history
  • Respect for every lender’s process and criteria

Approvals, loan amounts, and terms are always decided by the lender. Our role is to help you present a disciplined, documented picture of your business and your plan.

Square Bidness does not provide loans or make credit decisions. We help you prepare for conversations with lenders, including CDFIs and mission-driven institutions, so your business and plan are presented clearly and honestly.
