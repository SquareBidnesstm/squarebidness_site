Our Story

Square Bidness Apparel was founded to represent everyday people who handle business with pride and professionalism. From our first shirt in January 2022 to our growing operations today, we remain committed to quality, integrity, and innovation.

Veteran-led, community-driven, and scaling with purpose.

Our Values

Principles

We operate with unwavering integrity, treating every customer, partner, and team member with respect and fairness.

Morals

Our decisions are guided by doing what's right, not just what's profitable. We give back to the communities that built us.

Business Done Right

Quality products, honest pricing, and genuine relationships. We're building something that lasts.

Leadership

As a veteran-owned business, Square Bidness brings military values of discipline, integrity, and service to the apparel industry. Our Louisiana roots keep us grounded while our global vision drives us forward.
