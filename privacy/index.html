Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 24, 2025

At Square Bidness Apparel (“we”, “us”), your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Order Info: name, email, shipping/billing address, phone (if provided).
  • Payment: processed securely by our payment partner (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage Data: device, pages viewed, clicks, referring URLs, and cookies/analytics events (to improve the site).
  • Support: messages you send us (email, forms).

How We Use Information

  • Process and deliver orders; provide customer support.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and products.
  • Send updates and promotions if you opt in (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Prevent fraud and keep our services secure.

Payments

Transactions are handled by our payment processor (e.g., Stripe). They receive the payment information needed to process your order and are responsible for their own handling of that data.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand traffic and improve the experience. You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Learn more about Google’s data practices at policies.google.com.
